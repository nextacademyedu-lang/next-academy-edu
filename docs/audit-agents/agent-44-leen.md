## Evaluation Framework

For each platform, assess:

### Homepage UX
- Above-the-fold: What is the primary message? Is there a clear value proposition?
- CTA placement: Where is the primary "enroll / get started" button?
- Hero section: Does it communicate trust immediately?
- Load speed feel (subjective)

### Course/Program Page UX
- Social proof: Testimonials, ratings, student counts, logos?
- Instructor credibility: Bio, credentials, photo, video intro?
- Outcomes clarity: What will I be able to do after this course?
- Urgency/scarcity: "Only 5 seats left", "Enrollment closes Friday"?
- Sticky CTA: Does the enroll button follow as you scroll?

### Arabic/RTL Experience
- Is the Arabic version a true RTL design or just mirrored LTR?
- Is Arabic content well-written (formal MSA vs. Egyptian dialect)?
- Does the language switcher work intuitively?
- Are there any broken RTL layouts?

### Trust Signals
- Student review count and rating score
- Media logos ("As seen in...")
- Accreditation logos or certificates
- Money-back guarantee or free trial?
- Company registration / About page credibility

### Checkout / Enrollment Flow (if accessible without paying)
- How many clicks to reach checkout?
- Is registration required before seeing the price?
- Is the form minimal or overwhelming?
- Payment options visible?

### Social Media & Brand
- Facebook page: follower count, posting frequency, engagement (important in Egypt)
- Instagram presence
- YouTube channel: course previews, instructor videos
- Tone of voice: formal vs. friendly, Arabic vs. English

---

## Research Questions

1. **Trust-building** — Which competitor has the most credible, trust-building course page? What specific elements create that trust?
2. **Bilingual UX** — Which platform handles Arabic/English switching best? Is the Arabic experience equally polished?
3. **Social proof formats** — What formats are most common: written testimonials, video testimonials, ratings, student counts, logos?
4. **UX patterns missing from Next Academy** — Based on what you see competitors doing, what UX patterns should Next Academy adopt?
5. **Brand differentiation** — How does Next Academy's brand identity and voice compare to market leaders? Is it differentiated or generic?
