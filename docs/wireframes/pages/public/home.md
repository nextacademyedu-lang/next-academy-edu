# Wireframe: Homepage (`/`)

## 1. Hero Section

- **Title:** "Empower Your B2B Workforce"
- **Subtitle:** "Premium online courses and workshops led by industry experts."
- **CTA Buttons:** `Explore Programs` (Primary), `Talk to Sales` (Secondary).
- **Background:** Dynamic glow / glassmorphism effect.

## 2. Client Proof / Tech Stack

- **UI:** Horizontal auto-scrolling marquee of company logos (Notion, HubSpot, Shopify, etc.).

## 3. Promo Banner (الإعلانات أو الأحداث الهامة)

- **UI:** Full-width banner section with a distinct background color (e.g., Brand Gradient).
- **Content:** Headline (e.g., "Early Bird Registration Open!"), Subtext, and a CTA Button linking to a specific Round or Event.

## 4. Upcoming Events (كاروسيل للأحداث القادمة)

- **Header:** "Upcoming Schedule"
- **UI:** Tabs or Toggle Switch separating two main types (e.g., "Upcoming Workshops" | "Upcoming Webinars").
- **Content:** Interactive Carousel showing upcoming dates horizontally. Each item displays Date, Program Title, and `Book Now`/`Register` action.

## 5. Featured Programs

- **Header:** "Most Popular Programs".
- **Grid Layout:** 3 Cards showing top programs.
- **Card Content:** Category tag (Workshop/Course), Title, Instructor, Duration, Price, `View Details` button.

## 6. Corporate Training (تدريب الشركات)

- **UI:** A visually distinct section, perhaps with a split layout (Left: Text & CTA, Right: Illustration or Photo of a corporate team).
- **Header:** "Elevate Your Entire Team" or "Corporate Training Solutions".
- **Content:** "Customized learning paths, bulk enrollment discounts, and dedicated account management for B2B clients."
- **CTAs:** `Request a Custom Proposal` (Primary), `Download Brochure` (Secondary/Outline).

## 7. Why Choose Us?

- **Grid:** 4 Icons with short descriptions.
- **Points:** Expert Instructors, Flexible Installments, Actionable Material, B2B Focused.

## 8. Instructors Preview

- **Header:** "Learn From The Best".
- **Grid:** 4 Instructor profile cards (Photo, Name, Bio Snippet).

## 9. Testimonials

- **UI:** Carousel or grid of glassmorphic cards with user feedback.

## 10. Latest from the Blog (المقالات والأخبار)

- **Header:** "Insights & Articles"
- **Grid Layout:** 3 recent blog post cards.
- **Card Content:** Thumbnail Image, Published Date, Blog Title, Short summary, `Read More` link.
- **Action:** Master `View All Articles` button at the bottom or top right.
